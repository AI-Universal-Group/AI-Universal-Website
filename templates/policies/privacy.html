Privacy Policy

AI Universal ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • IP address
  • Device type

We process this information on the basis of user consent or for the purpose of fulfilling our contractual obligations.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate with you regarding our services and updates
  • To ensure the security of our services

We do not use your information for targeted marketing purposes, except for Google Ads. We use Google Ads to display advertisements to users who have previously visited our website. We do not store any data about the user, but Google will use targeted ads based on the user's browsing history and preferences.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Stripe for processing payments, MongoDB Atlas for securely storing user data, Google reCAPTCHA to prevent spam and abuse on our website, and Smartlook Analytics to analyze user behavior on our website. These third parties may collect, store, and use your personal information according to their respective privacy policies. We have entered into appropriate data processing agreements with these third parties to ensure the security and privacy of your data.

You can view the privacy policies of our third-party services here:

  • Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • MongoDB Atlas: https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google reCAPTCHA: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Smartlook Analytics: https://help.smartlook.com/docs/privacy-policy

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website, such as keeping you logged in and personalizing content. By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. You can read more about our cookies here.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. You also have the right to withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. To exercise these rights, you can email us or log in to our website and delete your account. Please note that deleting your account will invalidate any current subscription without any refund, and your subscription will automatically be canceled.

Security

We use encryption to protect the transmission of your personal information between your browser and our servers. We also use MongoDB Atlas to securely store your personal information in our database. We only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
