What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, track your online activity, and help websites function properly.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To keep you logged in to our website.
  • To personalize content based on your preferences.
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns using third-party services like Google Analytics and Smartlook Analytics.
  • To improve the performance and functionality of our website.
  • To serve advertisements based on your interests using Google Ads.
  • To manage tags and track website analytics using Google Tag Manager.
  • To prevent spam and abuse using Google reCAPTCHA.

Types of cookies we use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of our website, such as user authentication and navigation.
  • Performance cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website, allowing us to optimize and improve our services. We use third-party services like Google Analytics and Smartlook Analytics to collect this data, which may include information like your IP address, device type, and browsing history. This data is anonymized and aggregated to protect your privacy.
  • Functionality cookies: These cookies remember your preferences, such as language and display settings, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to serve advertisements based on your interests and browsing history using Google Ads.

You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, as well as to receive a notification before a cookie is stored on your device. Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.
